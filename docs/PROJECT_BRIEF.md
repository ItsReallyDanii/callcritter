# Project Brief — CallCritter

## Project name
**CallCritter**

## Public framing
A scene-aware AI companion generator for your real camera space.

## Elevator pitch
Open a browser link, use your camera or a demo scene, snap your desk/room, and CallCritter uses GPT-5.5 to understand the scene and Image Gen to create a tiny companion that belongs there. Place it on the scene and export a shareable card.

## Why this project
The OpenAI DevDay contest asks for something built with GPT-5.5 and Image Gen, submitted with a playable link and quick build note. CallCritter is optimized for that format: visual, fast, personal, and immediately understandable.

## Why not XylemForge for this contest
XylemForge is technically deeper but explanation-heavy. CallCritter is impressive before explanation and easier to judge in seconds.

## Core promise
GPT-5.5 does not just write copy. It reads the user’s actual scene, identifies mood/lighting/placement points, and generates the concept + image prompt. Image Gen then produces the companion asset.

## Contest priority order
1. Playable link.
2. Immediate visual clarity.
3. Visible GPT-5.5 reasoning.
4. Image Gen output as centerpiece.
5. Export/share artifact.
6. Strong docs.
7. Cost control.
